About

I am a Career Track Assistant Teaching Professor at the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence (SCAI) , Arizona State University (ASU) , starting Fall 2024. Previously, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at ASU (2022-2024). I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Graphics and Virtual Reality from Chung-Ang University in Seoul, South Korea (2018-2022). My research focuses on multimodal vision and artificial intelligence, particularly for intelligent transportation systems and 3D interactions in virtual reality (VR).
